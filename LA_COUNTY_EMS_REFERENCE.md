## PROCEDURES - SCOPE OF PRACTICE

### Confirmed Authorized Procedures
- Endotracheal Intubation (without RSI drugs)
- Defibrillation / Cardioversion
- Transcutaneous Pacing
- IV/IO Access
- 12-Lead ECG
- CPAP
- BVM Ventilation
- Supraglottic Airways (iGel, King)

### NOT AUTHORIZED - DO NOT INCLUDE
| Procedure | Status | Reason |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Needle Cricothyrotomy** | ❌ NOT USED | Confirmed by user - not in LA County protocols |
| **Surgical Cricothyrotomy** | ❌ NOT USED | Confirmed by user - not in LA County protocols |
| **RSI (Rapid Sequence Intubation)** | ❌ NOT CONFIRMED | Requires paralytic drugs not in formulary |

## PEDIATRIC DOSING

LA County uses **MCG 1309 Color Code Drug Doses** system:
- Based on Broselow length-based resuscitation tape
- Color zones correspond to weight ranges
- Pre-calculated doses in mL for each color zone
- Official app: LA County EMS Drug Doses

---

## DATA VALIDATION RULES FOR APP DEVELOPMENT

1. **ONLY** include medications from the approved LA County formulary
2. **NEVER** include Ketamine, Etomidate, Rocuronium, or Succinylcholine
3. **NEVER** include cricothyrotomy (needle or surgical)
4. **ALWAYS** cross-reference with RAPID LA County Medic app
5. **ALWAYS** use official LA County protocol reference numbers (Ref XXX, TP-XXXX)
6. **VERIFY** any new medications against official source before adding

---

## IMPORTANT NOTES

- Paramedics practice under **Off-line Medical Direction** via established protocols
- **Base Hospital Contact** required for treatments outside protocols
- All treatments must be **within paramedic scope** AND **evidence-based**
- California EMS Authority sets state scope; LA County has local adaptations
