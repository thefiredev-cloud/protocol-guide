## Compliance Considerations

### Medical App Guidelines

1. **No Diagnostic Claims**
   - Never claim app "diagnoses" conditions
   - Use language: "reference tool" and "supports clinical decision-making"
   - Clarify: "Not a substitute for medical training or judgment"

2. **FDA Mobile Medical App Guidance**
   - Protocol Guide is a reference tool, not a medical device
   - Does not control medical devices or provide patient-specific recommendations
   - Include disclaimer: "For educational and reference purposes"

3. **Accuracy Disclaimer**
   - "Protocols should be verified against your local/state guidelines"
   - "Always follow your medical director's standing orders"
   - Last updated date visible

### App Store Guidelines

1. **Health & Fitness Category Compliance**
   - Accurate representation of app functionality
   - No false claims about medical outcomes
   - Privacy policy for any health data

2. **Review Integrity**
   - Never incentivize reviews with app features
   - Respond to negative reviews professionally
   - Report and flag fraudulent competitor reviews

3. **Screenshot Accuracy**
   - All screenshots must reflect actual app UI
   - No misleading representations
   - Real protocol content (not placeholder text)

### HIPAA Considerations
- App does not collect PHI
- No patient data entry features
- Clear privacy policy stating no patient information stored
