## Growth Strategies

### Strategy 1: Viral Referral Program

#### "Crew Referral" Program
**Mechanic**: Every user gets unique referral code. Both referrer and referee benefit.

**Incentive Structure**:
- **Referrer receives**: 1 month Pro free per successful referral (stackable)
- **Referee receives**: 1 month Pro free trial (vs standard 7-day)
- **Bonus tier**: 5 referrals = 6 months Pro free
- **Super tier**: 10 referrals = 1 year Pro free

**Implementation**:
- In-app referral dashboard
- Easy share to text/WhatsApp/email
- "Share with your crew" prompt after positive interactions
- Partner-specific referral codes for tracking

**Viral Coefficient Target**: 1.2+ (each user brings 1.2 new users)

#### "Shift Share" Feature
- End of shift prompt: "Great shift? Share Protocol Guide with your partner"
- One-tap share with pre-written message
- Track partner-to-partner viral spread
- Gamification: "Protocol Guide Ambassador" badge

### Strategy 2: Educational Institution Pipeline

**Target**: EMT and Paramedic training programs nationwide

**Offer Structure**:

## Compliance Considerations

### Referral Program Compliance

1. **FTC Guidelines**
   - Clear disclosure of referral incentives
   - No misleading claims about benefits
   - Terms and conditions clearly stated
   - Proper tracking and reporting

2. **App Store Rules**
   - No incentives for reviews or ratings
   - Referral rewards for app usage only
   - Compliant with Apple/Google policies
   - No manipulation of download numbers

### B2B/Government Sales

1. **Procurement Compliance**
   - GSA Schedule eligibility (federal sales)
   - State purchasing cooperative participation
   - Proper bidding/RFP processes
   - Documentation for government contracts

2. **Data Security**
   - SOC 2 compliance for enterprise
   - CJIS compliance for law enforcement
   - No PHI storage
   - Clear data handling policies

### Medical Marketing

1. **Claims Restrictions**
   - No patient outcome claims
   - No "improves patient care" language without data
   - Reference tool positioning maintained
   - Disclaimers on all promotional materials

2. **Endorsement Guidelines**
   - Medical director endorsements properly disclosed
   - No implied endorsement from organizations
   - User testimonials genuine and documented
   - No celebrity medical endorsements

### Partnership Agreements

1. **Educational Partnerships**
   - Clear terms with institutions
   - Data sharing limitations
   - Student privacy (FERPA considerations)
   - Termination clauses

2. **Department Contracts**
   - Service level agreements
   - Data ownership clarity
   - Renewal and cancellation terms
   - Liability limitations
