# Searching for Protocols

Master the art of finding the exact protocol you need in seconds.

---

## How Search Works

Protocol Guide uses **AI-powered semantic search** to understand your questions and find relevant protocols. Unlike keyword-based search, you can ask questions naturally ‚Äì just like you'd ask a colleague.

### The Technology Behind It

1. **Your question** is converted into a numerical representation (embedding)
2. **55,000+ protocol chunks** are searched for semantic similarity
3. **AI (Claude)** reads the most relevant protocols
4. **A tailored response** is generated addressing your specific question

## Search Tips

### Be Specific

| Instead of... | Try... |
|---------------|--------|
| "Cardiac" | "Adult cardiac arrest treatment protocol" |
| "Drugs" | "Epinephrine dosing for anaphylaxis" |
| "Breathing" | "Management of respiratory distress in pediatric patients" |

### Include Patient Context

The AI provides better answers when you include:

- **Age group**: Adult, pediatric, geriatric, neonate
- **Condition**: Specific symptoms or diagnosis
- **Setting**: Field, ambulance, interfacility transport

**Examples**:
- "Pediatric seizure protocol for 5-year-old"
- "Geriatric fall assessment guidelines"
- "Neonatal resuscitation in the field"

### Ask Follow-Up Questions

Protocol Guide maintains conversation context. You can ask:

1. "What's the dose of adenosine for SVT?"
2. "What if the patient is pediatric?"
3. "What are the contraindications?"

Each follow-up refines the response based on previous context.

## Understanding Search Results

### Result Components

1. **AI Summary** (Top)
   - Direct answer to your question
   - Synthesized from multiple protocol sources
   - Includes dosages, steps, and considerations

2. **Confidence Indicator**
   - High confidence: Multiple matching protocols found
   - Medium confidence: Limited direct matches
   - Low confidence: Inferred from related protocols

3. **Source Documents** (Collapsible)
   - Original protocol text used for the response
   - Tap to expand and view full context
   - Shows which agency/state the protocol comes from

4. **Related Topics**
   - Suggested follow-up queries
   - Related protocols you might find useful

## Troubleshooting Search

### "No results found"

- Try rephrasing your question
- Use more general terms
- Check spelling of medical terms
- Ensure your region is selected correctly

### "Results don't match my question"

- Be more specific about the condition
- Include patient age/demographics
- Try asking in a different way

### Slow search results

- Check your internet connection
- Complex queries take longer (2-5 seconds)
- Peak usage times may be slower
