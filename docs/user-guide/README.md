## Installing Protocol Guide

Protocol Guide is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** ‚Äì no app store download required!

### On iPhone/iPad

1. Open Safari and go to **protocol-guide.com**
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** to confirm
5. Protocol Guide now appears on your home screen like a native app

### On Android

1. Open Chrome and go to **protocol-guide.com**
2. Tap the **three dots** menu (‚ãÆ)
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Add"** to confirm

### On Desktop (Windows/Mac)

1. Open Chrome and go to **protocol-guide.com**
2. Click the **install icon** in the address bar
3. Click **"Install"** in the popup
