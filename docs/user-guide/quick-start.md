## Step 2: Create an Account

1. Tap **"Sign In"** on the home screen
2. Choose your sign-in method:

3. Follow the prompts to authorize

Your account syncs your settings, bookmarks, and history across all devices.

## Step 4: Your First Search

### Type a Query

In the search bar, type a natural language question:

- "What's the dose of epinephrine for anaphylaxis?"
- "Pediatric cardiac arrest protocol"
- "STEMI treatment guidelines"
- "How do I treat a tension pneumothorax?"

Press **Enter** or tap the **Search** button.

### Or Use Voice Search

1. Tap the **microphone icon** (ğŸ¤)
2. Speak your question clearly
3. Wait for the transcription
4. Results appear automatically

---

## Step 5: Understanding Your Results

Protocol Guide returns:

1. **Direct Answer**: AI-generated summary addressing your specific question
2. **Source Protocols**: The original protocol documents used to generate the answer
3. **Related Protocols**: Other protocols that may be relevant

Tap any source protocol to view the full document.
