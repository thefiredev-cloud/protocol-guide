## Equipment Shot Guidelines

### Flat Lay Composition

**Setup:**
- Clean, solid background (white, light wood, or concrete)
- Soft, even lighting (near window or lightbox)
- Items arranged with breathing room
- Protocol Guide device at focal point

**Items to Include:**
- Stethoscope
- Trauma shears
- Penlight
- Pulse oximeter
- Glucometer
- Watch
- Phone with Protocol Guide open
- Notebook/pen
- Badge (optional, blur name)

**Composition Tips:**
- Grid or diagonal arrangement
- Leave space for text overlay
- Shoot from directly above
- Ensure all items are clean

### In-Action Shots

**Scenarios:**
- Training with mannequin (Protocol Guide visible)
- Rig check with device docked
- Station study session
- Conference/continuing education

**Guidelines:**
- No actual patients ever
- Mannequins clearly identifiable
- Clean, professional setting
- Natural device placement
