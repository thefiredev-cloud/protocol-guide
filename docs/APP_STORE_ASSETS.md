### Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Protocol Guide is the clinical decision support tool built for EMS professionals who need fast, reliable protocol access in the field.

INSTANT PROTOCOL SEARCH
Find the protocol you need in seconds. Our AI-powered semantic search understands medical terminology and returns relevant results even with partial queries. Ask "chest pain protocol" or "pediatric seizure dosing" and get immediate, citation-backed answers.

VOICE INPUT FOR HANDS-FREE ACCESS
On scene with gloves on? Use voice input to query protocols without touching your device. Protocol Guide transcribes your question and delivers the answer—perfect for high-pressure situations.

CITATION-BACKED RESPONSES
Every answer includes direct references to source protocols. No guessing, no ambiguity—just the information you need with clear citations you can verify.

OFFLINE ACCESS (PRO)
Downloaded protocols are available offline. Access your most-used references even in areas with poor connectivity—tunnels, rural areas, or underground parking structures.

COUNTY-SPECIFIC PROTOCOLS
Select your county and get protocols specific to your jurisdiction. Protocol Guide covers EMS agencies across all 50 states.

BUILT FOR THE FIELD
- Light theme optimized for outdoor visibility
- Minimal, scannable response format
- Quick action buttons for common emergencies
- Copy medication dosages with one tap
- Query history for fast re-access

FREE TIER
- 5 protocol queries per day
- 1 county
- Full search functionality
- Voice input

PRO SUBSCRIPTION ($4.99/month or $39/year)
- Unlimited queries
- All counties
- Offline protocol access
- Unlimited bookmarks
- Priority support

Protocol Guide is a reference tool designed to supplement—not replace—your training, local protocols, and medical direction. Always follow your agency's approved protocols and consult medical control when appropriate.

Questions? Contact support@protocol-guide.com
```

## App Store Optimization Tips

1. **Title**: Keep "Protocol Guide" as the primary name—clear and searchable
2. **Subtitle**: "EMS Protocol Reference" targets key search terms
3. **Keywords**: Focus on EMS-specific terms that competitors may miss
4. **Screenshots**: Lead with the value proposition, not the login screen
5. **Description**: Front-load benefits in the first 3 lines (visible before "Read More")
6. **Reviews**: Encourage satisfied users to leave reviews after successful queries
