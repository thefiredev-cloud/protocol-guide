## Competitive Analysis

### Alternatives Considered by Agencies

| Alternative | Pros | Cons |
|-------------|------|------|
| PDF Protocols | Familiar, free | Slow, outdated, no search |
| Mobile App (standalone) | Portable | Context switching, no ePCR link |

### Why Protocol Guide Wins

1. **Local Protocols**: Not generic national guidelines - actual local protocols
2. **AI-Powered Search**: Natural language search, not PDF keyword search
3. **HIPAA Compliant**: Built for healthcare from day one
4. **ePCR Integration**: Designed specifically for ePCR workflows
5. **Mobile-First**: Built for tablet/phone use in the field
